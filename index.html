About THREADS'26

THREADS'26 is a premier national-level technical symposium that converges innovation, expertise, and creativity. Join us for two transformative days of cutting-edge workshops, competitive events, and networking with industry pioneers.

Day 1 – Intensive Workshops

Dive deep into specialized workshops conducted by industry leaders and academic experts. Gain hands-on experience with emerging technologies and earn industry-recognized certifications.

  • Full-day immersive technical sessions
  • Industry-standard certifications
  • Practical projects & resource kits
  • Direct mentorship from experts

Day 2 – Competitions & Networking

Participate in exhilarating technical and non-technical competitions, showcase your innovations, and connect with like-minded peers and industry professionals.

  • National-level hackathons & coding challenges
  • Research paper presentations & project expo
  • Technical quizzes, gaming, and cultural events
  • Networking with recruiters and startups
